1. Data We Collect

1.1 Advertising Identifier (Advertising ID)

  • What: We collect your Google Advertising ID (AAID) when you use the App.

  • How: When you open the App, the Google Mobile Ads SDK automatically retrieves your AAID to request ads.

1.2 Device and Usage Information

  • What: Basic device details such as device model, operating system version, screen size, and App version.
  • Why: To ensure compatibility, measure performance, and optimize the user experience.
  • How: Collected automatically when you launch or use the App.

1.3 Payment Information (Razorpay)

  • What: When you purchase a background image (or any in-App purchase) via Razorpay, we collect:
    • Your email address (to send a receipt and confirm delivery of digital content).
    • Transaction ID and timestamp.
    • Partial billing address (only the data needed by Razorpay to process payment; we do not store your full card details).
  • Why: To process your purchase, send invoices or receipts, and ensure delivery of the digital asset you bought.
  • How: Payment is handled through Razorpay’s secure, PCI-compliant payment gateway. Razorpay collects your payment details (like card number or UPI information). We receive only the transaction confirmation and minimal buyer data.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • Personalize and Serve Ads: We use your Advertising ID to let Google Mobile Ads display targeted advertisements. Neither we nor Google collect personally identifiable information beyond the AAID for this purpose. You can reset or opt out of ads personalization at any time via your Android Settings: Android Settings → Google → Ads → “Opt out of Ads Personalization.”
  • Process In-App Purchases: When you buy a background image through Razorpay, we receive a confirmation containing your email and purchase ID. We use this to:
    • Deliver the purchased content within the App.
    • Send you an email receipt (if you provide an email).
    • Resolve any payment disputes or refund requests.
  • App Stability & Performance (Optional – if you use Crashlytics or Analytics): If we use Firebase Crashlytics (or any crash-reporting/analytics service), we collect crash logs and basic usage metrics (screen views, session duration, feature interactions) to improve stability. This data does not identify you personally. This collection is anonymous and used only for debugging and product improvements.
  • Support & Communication: If you contact us for support (e.g., regarding a failed payment or app issue), we will use the information you provide (such as your email address) to reply to your inquiry. We may send you transactional emails related to your purchase or account status but we will not send unsolicited marketing emails.
